\section{Error ID Reference}

All errors raised by the toolkit use the \texttt{FEAOPT:} prefix.
\Cref{tab:errors} lists the identifiers and their meanings.

\begin{table}[H]
\centering
\caption{Error identifiers.}
\label{tab:errors}
\begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{lX}
\toprule
\textbf{ID} & \textbf{Meaning} \\
\midrule
\texttt{FEAOPT:badInput}         & Unexpected input type to a constructor or method. \\
\texttt{FEAOPT:badSize}          & Array dimension mismatch. \\
\texttt{FEAOPT:badMemberID}      & Element ID out of range. \\
\texttt{FEAOPT:badNodeID}        & Node ID out of range. \\
\texttt{FEAOPT:badDOF}           & Global DOF index out of range. \\
\texttt{FEAOPT:badConnectivity}  & Connectivity references invalid node. \\
\texttt{FEAOPT:badConstraint}    & Constraint references invalid node or DOF. \\
\texttt{FEAOPT:badOption}        & Unrecognised option string. \\
\texttt{FEAOPT:zeroLength}       & Zero-length element detected. \\
\texttt{FEAOPT:nonPositive}      & Non-positive material or section property. \\
\texttt{FEAOPT:noConstraints}    & No boundary conditions applied (warning). \\
\texttt{FEAOPT:noOptimizable}    & No non-rigid elements available to optimise. \\
\texttt{FEAOPT:noActiveElements} & All elements filtered out in profile export. \\
\texttt{FEAOPT:noAnalysis}       & Plotter method requires an analysis object. \\
\texttt{FEAOPT:notConverged}     & Newton--Raphson did not converge (warning). \\
\texttt{FEAOPT:numericalFailure} & NaN or Inf in displacement increment (warning). \\
\texttt{FEAOPT:negativeLambda}   & Negative load factor applied (warning). \\
\texttt{FEAOPT:lowRigidFactor}   & Rigid stiffness factor $\leq 1$ (warning). \\
\texttt{FEAOPT:tooFewPoints}     & CSV file has fewer than 2 points. \\
\texttt{FEAOPT:noEntities}       & No supported entities found in IGES file. \\
\texttt{FEAOPT:noData}           & No valid curve data extracted from IGES. \\
\texttt{FEAOPT:chainFailed}      & Could not chain IGES entities. \\
\texttt{FEAOPT:chainGap}         & Gap detected between IGES entities (warning). \\
\texttt{FEAOPT:fileWrite}        & Cannot open file for writing. \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabularx}
